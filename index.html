How to Play :

Start: Players face each other, often holding one hand as a fist on top of their open palm. They say "Rock, paper, scissors, shoot" in unison. Throw: On the word "shoot," both players simultaneously form one of the three shapes with their other hand.

Determine the winner: The outcome is decided based on the following rules: Rock crushes scissors: A player who chooses rock beats a player who chooses scissors. Scissors cuts paper: A player who chooses scissors beats a player who chooses paper. Paper covers rock: A player who chooses paper beats a player who chooses rock.

Tie: If both players choose the same shape, it's a tie, and the players must play again until there is a winner.
